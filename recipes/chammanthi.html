Description

CHammanthii is a South Indian dish, predominantly used in the state of Kerala

Ingredients

  • thenga
  • tamarind
  • chilly powder
  • salt

Steps

  1. scrape the thenga in really small size
  2. add salt and one teaspoon water
  3. add mulak podi
  4. add tamarind
  5. mix and serve
